How to Play Double Trouble

Double Trouble is played with two players. Each player starts with two game pieces in their safe places.

Players take turns clicking the pop-o-matic die in the center of the game board to reveal a number for which to move their piece.

Be careful for double trouble! If another player's piece lands on yours, your piece gets returned to its starting position.

The first player to move both pieces around the game board and return them back to their safe places wins.

Have fun and remember.. don't get into Double Trouble!
